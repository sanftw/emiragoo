10 Unique UAE Experiences to Try at Least Once

By EmiraGo

The UAE is more than just glitzy malls and towering skyscrapers—it's a vibrant blend of tradition, innovation, and adventure. Whether you're a tourist or a long-time resident, these 10 unique experiences promise to show you a different side of the Emirates.

  1. Desert Safari at Sunset
    Ride across golden dunes in a 4x4, try sandboarding, and enjoy a traditional Bedouin-style dinner under the stars.
  2. Visit the Al Fahidi Historical Neighborhood
    Explore narrow lanes, wind towers, and restored homes that showcase Dubai's humble beginnings before oil.
  3. Kayak Through Mangroves in Abu Dhabi
    Paddle your way through serene mangrove forests in Jubail Island, a surprising escape into nature.
  4. Ride the World’s Fastest Rollercoaster
    Head to Ferrari World in Yas Island and experience the Formula Rossa reaching speeds of up to 240 km/h.
  5. Stay Overnight in the Mountains
    Camp in Ras Al Khaimah’s Jebel Jais mountains for cool temperatures and incredible sunrise views.
  6. Take a Traditional Abra Ride
    For just 1 AED, cross the Dubai Creek on a wooden boat and soak in the contrast between old souks and modern towers.
  7. Watch Camel Racing at Al Marmoom
    Witness the unique sport of camel racing, complete with robot jockeys and a festive atmosphere.
  8. Explore Ghost Towns in the Desert
    Visit abandoned villages like Al Madam in Sharjah—a haunting reminder of life in the desert before urbanization.
  9. Taste a Local Home-Cooked Meal
    Platforms like Frying Pan Adventures or local cultural centers offer authentic Emirati dining experiences.
  10. Attend a Cultural Workshop
    Learn about henna, Arabic calligraphy, or falconry at the Sheikh Mohammed Centre for Cultural Understanding.
